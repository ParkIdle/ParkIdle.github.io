F.A.Q.

Does this app work like other parking apps?
No, this is a unique project: we want help people at finding an empty parking spot using only their GPS.
Each user who will have the app running, communicates his status with our servers,so everyone can receive updates about others!
For example: if I am leaving a parking spot, others will receive a notification about my departure, that's how they find where to park.
The most users use ParkIdle,the better the app will work!

Where can I use ParkIdle?
You can use it everywhere,
but as mentioned above, it will work better where there are people who use it.

How does the app works?
The app uses the information of the users, showing the empty parking spots near you:
open the app and you will find some coloured pin near you on the map, they will show you the empty parking spots, just tap on them and follow the instruction.

Does this app work on my phone?
At first we'll realease the App on Android but in the future we will develop an iOS version :) .
