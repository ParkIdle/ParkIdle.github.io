One idea,
million of problems solved.

ok, maybe we are magnifying our app a little bit.

We are four students from Rome.
We struggle everyday for finding parking solution, so it's time to start a little revolution.

The idea is based on the future concept of smart cities, where "smart" objects help us with nowdays problems, providing a easier resolution.
Our application is basically similar every other ordinary maps application, beside

one detail:

It shows you everyone who's around you and is going away from a parking spot.

intuitive and simple, you can check which spot is going to be empty, select it and we'll provide you fastest direction to get there.

Pretty simple, hum?
