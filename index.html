How to install widgets on iOS

  • 1. Swipe right over the Home screen or Lock screen.
  • 2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Edit.
  • 3. To add a widget, tap +. To remove a widget, tap -.
        To reorder your widgets, touch and hold next to the apps and drag them in the order that you want.
  • 4. To finish, tap Done.
